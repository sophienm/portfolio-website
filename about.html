My Story

My curiosity of other peoples' perspectives and my need to understand human behaviours and expectations fuel my desire to create meaningful experiences. After years of working as a fashion designer, I felt the need for change and decided to pursue a career in UX Design where I can design experiences for specific persons and needs and I feel less like I am designing simply for aesthetics.

My previous experiences taught me how important it is to have an understanding of what the teams you work with do. It simplifies communication and it helps to create a great product efficiently. This pushed me to continuously educate myself and has allowed me to wear multiple hats. And I plan on continuing to do so in the future. FYI I developed this website.
